Our Heritage

A Legacy of Design Excellence Since 1998

Founded in London by visionary architects, ARCHVISTA STUDIO began with a simple yet profound mission: to create spaces that elevate the human spirit.

Over the past 25+ years, we have grown from a boutique firm into a global powerhouse in luxury architecture, urban planning, and interior design. Our portfolio spans 15 countries, featuring everything from avant-garde residential villas to sustainable corporate headquarters.

We combine rigorous structural engineering with fluid, emotional design to produce landmarks that stand the test of time.

Elena Rostova, Founder
